About The Event

Yuri’s Night Celebration 2026 is an annual event organized by the St. Xavier’s Physics Council Nepal (SXPC-Nepal) to commemorate the historic first human spaceflight of Yuri Gagarin on April 12, 1961. The event aims to celebrate achievements in space exploration while inspiring students to engage with astronomy, astrophysics, and space science. The program will include interactive activities such as solar observation, scientific talks, space-themed performances, VR experiences, games, exhibitions, and night sky observation. Through these activities, the event will foster curiosity about physics and promote a culture of scientific learning within the college community.
